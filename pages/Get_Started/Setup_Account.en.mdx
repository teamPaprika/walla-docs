## Profile Setup, Changing Names, and Delete Account

<Steps>
    ### Click Profile Picture in the upper right corner to access profile settings

    ![Untitled](https://paprikastorage.com/images%2FsetupAccount%2Fen%2F3.png)

    ### Click 'Upload Image' to change your profile image. You can change your name under 'Display Name'.
    - The registered email address cannot be changed.

    ![Untitled](https://paprikastorage.com/images%2FsetupAccount%2Fen%2F4.png)

    ### If you wish to delete the account, click 'Delete Account'
    - Deleted accounts are difficult to recover. Please be cautious about deleting accounts.

    ![Untitled](https://paprikastorage.com/images%2FsetupAccount%2Fen%2F5.png)

</Steps>
