## Sharing Project (Step View vs List View)


<Steps>
    ### Click 'Share' in the left navigation bar.

    ![Untitled](/images/createForm&surveyAndShare/sharingProject/en/0.png)

    ### You can either choose 'Step View' or 'List View' for sharing the survey with respondents.

    ![Untitled](/images/createForm&surveyAndShare/sharingProject/en/1.png)

    - Step View: One question per page.
    - List View: All questions on one page (like Google Forms). See the example image below.
    - Pick the view format that fits your survey best: Step View or List View.

    ![Untitled](/images/createForm&surveyAndShare/sharingProject/en/2.png)

    Step View Sample

    ![Untitled](/images/createForm&surveyAndShare/sharingProject/en/3.png)

    List View Sample

    ### Curious about how the survey looks to respondents? Click 'Survey shortcut' to check it out.

    ![Untitled](/images/createForm&surveyAndShare/sharingProject/en/4.png)
</Steps>


## Customize Shared Images and Links

<Steps>
    - When sharing the link via KakaoTalk or other social media platforms, you can customize the preview image, link, title, and description visible to survey respondents.

    ![Untitled](/images/createForm&surveyAndShare/sharingProject/en/5.png)

    ### Add image, link, title, and description. Click 'Save' to store.

    - Enter the link according to the format. Click the blue exclamation mark button next to 'Link Customization' to check the restrictions.

    ![Untitled](/images/createForm&surveyAndShare/sharingProject/en/6.png)

    - The link will be shared as follows!

    ![Untitled](/images/createForm&surveyAndShare/sharingProject/en/7.png)

</Steps>

<Callout type='warning' emoji="⚠️">**Customizing the link defaults to 'Step View' (as of February 14, 2023). The List View + link customization feature is coming soon!**</Callout>
<Callout type='warning' emoji="⚠️">**Link Customization Restrictions**</Callout>
    - Enter between 0 and 30 characters.
    - No slashes (/) allowed.
    - Cannot consist of only one or two periods (.).
    - Cannot match the regular expression (***).
