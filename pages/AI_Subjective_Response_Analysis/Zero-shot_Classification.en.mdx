## Zero-shot Classification

<Callout type='info' emoji="ðŸ””">**Zero-shot Classification** classifies responses based on given classification
    criteria.</Callout>

<Steps>
    ### Select the item you want to classify.
    - Only responses to items with short answer, long answer, numerical, and subjective table questions can be analyzed.

    ![image](https://paprikastorage.com/images%2FaiSubjectiveResponseAnalysis%2Fen%2F3.png)

    ### Specify the classification criteria for the responses.
    - Classify by the Enter key as a boundary.
    - Each item is included in at least one classification group, and you can verify the prediction accuracy through the
    score.

    ![image](https://paprikastorage.com/images%2FaiSubjectiveResponseAnalysis%2Fen%2F4.png)

    ![image](https://paprikastorage.com/images%2FaiSubjectiveResponseAnalysis%2Fen%2F5.png)

</Steps>
