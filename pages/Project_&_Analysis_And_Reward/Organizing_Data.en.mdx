## Organizing Data with Group-By Function

<Callout type='info' emoji="üîî">**For instance, use the group-by function to group data from a multiple-choice question with 'Agree' and 'Yes, I agree' options.**</Callout>

## Using Group-By Function

- Imagine conducting a survey to identify popular destinations for Jeju Air with the following questions.
- If you want to group data based on 'country,' you can use the group-by function.

![Untitled](https://paprikastorage.com/images%2ForganizingData%2Fen%2F0.png)

<Steps>
    ### Go to 'Dashboard'.

    ![Untitled](https://paprikastorage.com/images%2ForganizingData%2Fen%2F1.png)

    ### Click 'Group Settings' in the top right corner of the card.

    ![Untitled](https://paprikastorage.com/images%2ForganizingData%2Fen%2F2.png)

    ### Click the '+Add' button in the group-by window.

    ![Untitled](https://paprikastorage.com/images%2ForganizingData%2Fen%2F3.png)

    ### Drag and drop the item you want to group to create a single group.
    - For example, you can group Tokyo, Osaka, and Nagoya into a group called 'Japan.' To do this, let's first drag 'Tokyo' and drop it onto 'Osaka'.

    ![Untitled](https://paprikastorage.com/images%2ForganizingData%2Fen%2F4.png)

    - Tokyo has been placed under Osaka successfully. Now, let's also place 'Nagoya' under Osaka.

    ![Untitled](https://paprikastorage.com/images%2ForganizingData%2Fen%2F5.png)

    - Under Osaka includes 'Osaka,' 'Tokyo,' and 'Nagoya.' Now, let's change the title to 'Japan'.

    ![Untitled](https://paprikastorage.com/images%2ForganizingData%2Fen%2F6.png)

    - Once the changes are made, the 'Japan' group will appear on the screen.

    ![Untitled](https://paprikastorage.com/images%2ForganizingData%2Fen%2F7.png)

    - Since 'Tokyo' and 'Nagoya' have no subvalues, click the 'üëÅ' to hide them.

    ![Untitled](https://paprikastorage.com/images%2ForganizingData%2Fen%2F8.png)

    ### You can see that under the 'Japan' item, the values 'Tokyo,' 'Nagoya,' and 'Osaka' are grouped together.

    ![Untitled](https://paprikastorage.com/images%2ForganizingData%2Fen%2F9.png)

</Steps>
